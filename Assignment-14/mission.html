The Mission of Why Greens

The end goal of this website would be to used as education as to not only why greens are so important in todays diet, but also to share ways to create new dishes or try new tastes for anyone less than thrilled by the idea of vegitetables. In a world where everything has to taste good but everyone seems to not feel good for some reason or another, would you trade taste for feeling good more often? Extenuating circumstances aside.. There are thousands of ways to eat vegetables that taste good and make you feel good to boot!
