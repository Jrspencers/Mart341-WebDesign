About Me

As a kid I was always forced to eat my vegetetables growing up, or at least most of them like. I was not a big fan like almost all kids. Looking back, I am glad that this happened to me though. As I've grown older, I'd say probably close to 50% of the people I know don't care for vegetables. Some try, some won't try, but most of them seem to have some sort of dietary issue. I like knowing how things work, and if I can, I would like to share my knowledge to help others find out how they can improved themselves through the addition of vegetables to their current diet. *Disclaimer: Greens work great alone or even better with sources of proteins (meats) and healthy omega 3s(fish) and other fats(legumes/avocados)*
